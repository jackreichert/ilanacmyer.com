I’m excited to announce that I will be celebrating the launch of *Fire Dance* at three fantastic indie bookstores in the US and Canada.

The schedule:

-   [Borderlands Books](https://borderlands-books.com/) in San Francisco on Saturday, April 14th at 3pm
-   [Kew and Willow Books](http://www.thequeensbookshop.com/) in Queens, New York on Sunday, April 22nd at 5:30pm
-   [Bakka Phoenix Books](http://www.bakkaphoenixbooks.com/) in Toronto on Sunday, April 29th at 3pm

I’ll be reading, signing, and happy to sign copies of *Last Song Before Night* as well. See you there!
