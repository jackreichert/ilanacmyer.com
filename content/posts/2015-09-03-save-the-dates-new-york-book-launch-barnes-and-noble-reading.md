I have lots of tour news, but first up:

-   The book launch! It will be **October 1st at 7pm** in the [Red Room](http://redroomnyc.com/) above the KGB bar, and will be in collaboration with *Traitor Baru Cormorant* author [Seth Dickinson](http://www.sethdickinson.com/). The event is titled, “Songs of Rebellion: A Reading with Seth Dickinson and Ilana C. Myer.”

-    On **October 3rd at 3pm** I will be reading in the Barnes & Noble in Forest Hills, New York.

So people in the New York area have a choice which event to attend! I would love to see you at one of these.

Tor.com has the details of our [northeast book tour](http://www.tor.com/2015/09/03/seth-dickinson-fran-wilde-ilana-myer-fall-flights-of-fantasy/) here, and  details for New York Comic Con are forthcoming.
