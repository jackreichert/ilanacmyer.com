No, the book isn’t out yet, but it *is* available on Amazon for pre-order: [Last Song Before Night](http://www.amazon.com/Last-Song-Before-Night-Ilana/dp/0765378302/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1421692612&sr=8-1&keywords=last+song+before+night&pebp=1421692615891&peasin=765378302).

Official release date will be in September of this year.

And this is fun–my piece for the *Los Angeles Review of Books* about [Tyrion from *Game of Thrones*](http://lareviewofbooks.org/essay/need-talk-tyrion-hbo-failed-george-r-r-martins-iconic-character) nearly tops the list of the site’s most-read pieces in 2014. [I’m #2!](http://lareviewofbooks.org/essay/larb-top-25-read-articles-2014) It was a piece people seemed to either love or hate, but what pleases me most is that it generated intense discussion–which I believe indicates the argument was resonant for many.
